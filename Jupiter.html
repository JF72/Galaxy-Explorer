Hello from Jupiter!


Fun Facts about Me

  • I was named after the king of the Roman Gods, Jupiter
  • A day here is only 10 hours, but a year here is 11.9 Earth years
  • My distance from the Sun is 741 million km
  • At my closest I'm 592 million km from Earth and at my furthest I'm 965.5 million km from Earth
  • Although you can't see it, I do have rings
  • I have the most moons in our solar system at 92, my most well known ones are Europa and Io
  • Unlike the first four planets, I am a gas giant which means I have no surface to land on
  • A storm called the Great Red Spot has been on me for centuries
  • I'm mostly made of hydrogen and helium
