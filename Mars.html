Hello from Mars!


Fun Facts about Me

  • I was named after the Roman God of war, Mars
  • A day here is about 25 hours long, while a year here is 687 Earth days
  • I have two moons, Phobos and Deimos
  • Similar to Earth, I have water ice caps under my north and south poles
  • Unlike sunsets on Earth, my sunsets are blue due to the composition of my atmosphere
  • My atmosphere is mostly made of carbon dioxide, argon, and nitrogen
  • Several rovers are currently on and acitve on my surface
  • Olympus Mons is the tallest mountain in the solar system at 16 miles tall, 3 times the height of Mt. Everest
